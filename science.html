On Monday, we did an experiment with glass jars, their lids, hot water, and hair spray. We filled the jars with the water, and Mr. Shortt added a quick dah of hair spray. Then we put the lids on upside down, and filled them with ice. The ice chilled the lid, so the hot water evaporated and formed water vapor around the hair spray particles. When we opened the jar, the water vapor floated out. On Tuesday, we read an article about energy and its different forms. We learned about kinetic energy, potential energy, mechanical energy, and their uses in everyday life. Then we each wrote a paragraph about what we learned about energy. On Wednesday, we did a lab on thermal energy. Each group had a plastic cup filled with cold water. One person put their fingers in the water, and we measured the change in water temperature after 6 minutes. Then, they coated their fingers in crisco, and we measured the change in water temperature after 6 minutes.
